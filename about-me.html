About me

Hello I'm Nicole and I am so happy you have found my page. I'm a fun loving girl with a big heart. I love to touch, pamper, and really connect with people that come into my life. I created the pamper hotel because I saw that there was a disconnect between the service we get at a high class spa and the human touch that that is natural and required for most people. I would love to welcome you into my home, give you that amazing massage you have been reading about, provide you with the best of cuisine, but also offer you my full attention and presence to really get give you want you are wanting, craving, or just like to try while you stay at my place. I am a trained massage therapist, social worker, counsellor, life coach, and activity co-coordinator. However, those are just words. I invite you to come see me for an evening, weekend or even a week, and see what it feels like to be pampered mind body and soul.
